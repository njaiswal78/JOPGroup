Career with Us

JOP is an organization of zealous engineers and space planers, rich in experience and high on commitment, which has helped the group to sustain professional excellence for more than 2 decades. It is a 25-year old real estate developer having completed 100+ projects in Delhi NCR. JOP has footprints across all verticals be it malls, townships, residential and group housings. It is North India’s most geographically diverse real estate developers with properties in Delhi, Noida, Rohtak, Ambala, Narnaul and Yamuna Nagar.

At JOP we are committed to provide a lifestyle backed by the highest standards of construction, design ethics, product quality and the best of amenities and facilities. The company is currently diversifying in an all-new exciting space which will touch and captivate customers across India and the world.
