  • Yamunanagar is poised for a life style make over

    First mall in Yamunanagar with multiplex, shopping & entertainment zone Prominently located in the city epicenter, on Jagadhri - Yamuna Nagar Road Surrounded by reputed colleges, schools, 3-star and other good hotels etc. Also a stop-over place for the tourists visiting Haridwar, Rishikesh, Dehradun. Total constructed area appx. 90,000 sq. ft.

    For a grand shopping experience

    JOP Square, Yamunanagar with its domineering charisma and regal facade will soon emerge as the most favourite hangout place and shopping destination for both shopaholics and serious buyers. As the confluence of high profile retailers and discerning buyers, it will have stylish & flashing shops and showrooms where brands will pop out from the glass doors & windows to engage visitors and influence them to buy. The subtle, sophisticated, soothing & comforting environment will make it a fun-filled spot for people across age groups. A heady mix of both national & international brands will engulf one and all, and toss them in a passionate extravagance.
